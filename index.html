About Us

The Gilbertstone Gladiators started as a grassroots basketball community based in Sheldon. A collection of players from the surrounding area slowly trickled in over the weeks of lockwdown and came together during COVID lockdown as a way to pass the time, get some exercise and smoke some tree.

Team News

Gilberststone beat local rivals 112-70

The Gladiaors beat Yardley 112-70 in last weeks fixture. The team saw a season high performance from Player A, who led the team in scoring, rebounds and assists with a 18-11-10 triple double.

For those watching ti was clear the team has found it's footing and are really clicking on offense with a variety of structured plays and excellent 1 v 1 isolations, highlights of this weeks game will be available on our Youtube channel this coming week!
